HOW IT WORKS

A mutual exchange.

FOR OUR CLUB
We feature your project
in our portfolio.

Every design we deliver may be showcased as an example of our work, credited to Design Bridge Club (The Creative Arch). This helps us grow, improve, and show the campus what we're capable of.

FOR YOU
You get a professional design,
free of charge.

Use our design for your artwork, events, or projects — just credit Design Bridge Club on your final materials. Your work gets elevated. Our club gets recognized. Everyone wins.

!
REVISION POLICY

We're committed to getting your design right. After delivery, you have one week to request revisions. After this window, we may not be able to accommodate changes due to our team's schedule. Please review your files carefully and share feedback promptly.
